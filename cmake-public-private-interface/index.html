cmake: public private interface

Ben July 25, 2021 cmake

CMake Access:

add_library(mylib mylib.cpp)
target_include_directoires(
    PUBLIC ${CMAKE_CURRENT_SOURCE_DIR}/include
)

add_executable(myExe main.cpp)
target_link_libraries(myExe PRIVATE mylib)

Explain:
1. include files in mylib is for both mylib and its dependency myExe

2. mylib is only used by myExe

NameMeaning
PRIVATENeeded by me, but NOT by my dependers
PUBLICNeeded by me and my dependers
INTERFACENOT needed by me, but needed by my dependers
